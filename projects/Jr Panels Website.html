    Project Title: Jr Panel Website


    Overview:


    I developed a professional website for JR Panels, a company specializing in built-in cupboards, shop fitting, laminated flooring, glass splash art, and more. The website features a comprehensive About Us section, detailed descriptions of services, a photo gallery showcasing their work, and essential contact information.


    Objectives:

    The main objectives were to create an online presence for JR Panels, highlight their expertise and services, and provide a user-friendly platform for potential clients to learn about the company and get in touch.


    Challenges Overcome:

    • Responsive Design:Ensuring the website is accessible and looks great on all devices.
    • Content Organization:Effectively organizing and presenting a large amount of information in a clean, easy-to-navigate format.
    • SEO Optimization:Implementing best practices for search engine optimization to increase visibility and reach.

    Technologies Used:

    • HTML:For the structure of the website.
    • CSS:For styling and layout
    • JavaScript:For interactive elements and enhanced user experience.
